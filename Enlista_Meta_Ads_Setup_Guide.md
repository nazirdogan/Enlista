## Action Items Before Publishing

### You Need To Do Manually:

1. **Accept Lead Generation Terms of Service** (Error #1815089)
   - Go to Enlista.io Facebook Page Settings → Click "View Terms" → Accept Meta's Lead Gen ToS
   - This is required before Campaign 2 (Enterprise Leads) can run

2. **Add Payment Method**
   - Go to Enlista.io Ad Account → Payment Settings → Add a credit/debit card
   - Required before any ads can be published

3. **Upload Ad Creative Images/Videos**
   - Campaign 1: Upload your Enlista product image/video to the "60-Second Listing" ad
   - Campaign 2: Upload your Enterprise-focused image to the "ROI Calculator" ad
   - **Important for Campaign 2:** When you upload the image, the creative wizard will open. Go to the "Text" tab and enter the ad copy provided above, set CTA to "Get quote"

4. **Activate Meta Pixel**
   - A pixel exists on the account but has no events detected
   - Install the pixel on enlista.io to track conversions

5. **Connect Instagram Account** (Optional)
   - No Instagram account is currently connected to the Enlista.io page
   - Connect one in Page Settings → Instagram to run ads on Instagram

6. **Verify Phone Number**
   - Account notification indicates phone verification is needed

### After Completing The Above:
- Review both campaigns in "Review and publish" mode
- Toggle campaigns from Draft to Active
- Monitor performance in Ads Manager
