## Tips for Meta Approval

- **Don't edit the template after submission** — changes require re-approval.
- If a template is rejected, the most common reason is "too promotional." The fix is usually softening the opening line to feel more personal and less like a cold ad.
- Templates 1 and 4 are the strongest for approval because they open with a question or insight, not a pitch.
- Make sure your WATI account has your **business display name** matching your Meta Business account to avoid delays.
