Tone of Voice — Do & Don't

✓ Do
Specific numbers always. "<4 min avg. publish time" not "fast." "124K listings managed" not "thousands." Precision earns trust.
✓ Do
Industry-native language. "Portal", "RERA", "DLD", "per sqft", "Bayut", "PF." Write as if you work in UAE real estate.
✓ Do
Short, direct sentences. "Transparent pricing. No surprises." mirrors the site's own copy voice — confident, no fluff.
✕ Don't
No gold, no luxury aesthetic. Enlista is B2B SaaS. Blue and clean. Not desert gold and dark luxury.
✕ Don't
No "AI revolution" language. The site says "AI handles the rest" — quiet confidence, not hype.
✕ Don't
No other fonts. Plus Jakarta Sans only. No Syne, no serif, no display type. One font = one brand.
