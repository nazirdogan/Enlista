# Enlista Instagram Reels — Design Specification
**Date:** April 12, 2026  
**Project:** Enlista (formerly ListingAI)  
**Scope:** 4 Instagram Reels (30–45 seconds each) showcasing core product value  

---

## Overview

Create 4 high-energy Instagram Reels that demonstrate Enlista's core capabilities:
1. **Voice-to-Copy Workflow** — The product demo showing real voice input → instant bilingual output
2. **WhatsApp Automation** — Instant, personalized lead follow-ups
3. **Speed/Time Savings** — Transformation from 90+ minutes to 60 seconds per listing
4. **Lead Response Race** — Competitive edge: respond first, book the meeting first

Each Reel will be authentic, benefit-focused, and optimized for Instagram's 16:9 portrait format (1080×1920px, 30–45 seconds). The reels will blend snappy animation with real product interface screenshots and voice synthesis to create hyper-realistic demos.

---

## Reel Specifications

### Reel 1: Voice-to-Copy Workflow
**Duration:** 35 seconds  
**Format:** 1080×1920px (16:9 portrait)  
**Hook:** "One voice note. Everything ready in seconds."

**Visual Flow:**
1. **Opening (0–3 sec)** — Animated microphone button with pulsing glow
2. **Voice Input (3–12 sec)** — Agent speaks property details (11 Labs Rachel voice playing audio)
   - Real audio: Property description about Arabian Ranches villa
   - Transcription text animates on-screen in real-time
3. **Generation Phase (12–25 sec)** — Listing cards populate with generated content
   - English copy slides in from left
   - Arabic (Bilingual) copy slides in from right
   - Show checkmarks next to completed elements (title, description, price, features)
4. **Multi-Channel Ready (25–32 sec)** — Final output with icons for each platform
   - "Ready for Bayut" ✓
   - "Ready for Dubizzle" ✓
   - "Ready for Instagram" ✓
   - "Ready for WhatsApp" ✓
5. **CTA (32–35 sec)** — Text overlay: "Try free at enlista.ai"

**Audio:**
- 11 Labs voice (Rachel) — property description (pre-generated, synced to animation)
- Background music — upbeat, modern, energetic (emphasizes speed/innovation)

**Text Overlays:**
- "Say it once."
- "Everything ready in seconds."
- "English + Arabic."
- "Copy-paste ready."
- CTA: "Try free at enlista.ai"

**Animation Techniques:**
- Microphone pulse (CSS animation)
- Text typing effect (character-by-character reveal)
- Card slide-ins with stagger effect
- Icon checkmarks with bounce animation

### Reel 2: WhatsApp Automation
**Duration:** 40 seconds  
**Format:** 1080×1920px (16:9 portrait)  
**Hook:** "Your lead replies. You respond instantly."

**Visual Flow:**
1. **Setup (0–4 sec)** — iPhone mockup positioned on-screen with WhatsApp interface visible
2. **Incoming Message (4–8 sec)** — Lead inquiry slides into chat
   - Example: "Hi, I'm interested in that villa. Can you send more details?"
   - Message notification animation
3. **Auto-Generation (8–20 sec)** — Enlista generates personalized response in real-time
   - Typing bubble appears
   - Generated message shows character-by-character (simulated typing)
   - Message appears in conversation with property details, photos, link
4. **Scenario Variations (20–32 sec)** — Show 2 more quick scenarios
   - Different property type → different personalized response
   - Fast cuts between scenarios (demonstrating versatility)
5. **Impact Statement (32–38 sec)** — Text overlay
   - "No more delays."
   - "No more lost leads."
   - "Your leads stay warm."
6. **CTA (38–40 sec)** — "Contact us to set up automation"

**Audio:**
- Subtle notification sound (message incoming)
- Background music — conversational, warm, professional tone
- No voiceover (let the visual demo speak)

**Text Overlays:**
- "Lead comes in."
- "You respond instantly."
- "Personalized. Every time."
- "No delays. No lost leads."
- CTA: "Contact us for setup"

**Animation Techniques:**
- iPhone frame (CSS/mockup positioned on canvas)
- Message bubble slide-in
- Typing animation (cursor blinking, text appearing)
- Fade transitions between scenarios
- Emphasis on speed (quick cuts, fast animations)

### Reel 3: Speed/Time Savings
**Duration:** 38 seconds  
**Format:** 1080×1920px (16:9 portrait)  
**Hook:** "From 90 minutes to 60 seconds."

**Visual Flow:**
1. **Opening (0–3 sec)** — Split-screen setup
   - Left: "Without Enlista" (red tint background)
   - Right: "With Enlista" (green tint background)
2. **Left Side: Manual Process (3–20 sec)** — Time counter 90:00 → 60:00
   - Shows staggered tasks ticking by:
     - "Writing property description... 15 min"
     - "Translating to Arabic... 20 min"
     - "Creating Instagram caption... 10 min"
     - "Generating WhatsApp message... 5 min"
   - Counter ticks slowly, visually showing wasted time
   - Tasks appear one by one with checkmarks
3. **Right Side: Enlista Process (3–20 sec)** — Time counter 1:00 → 0:30
   - All tasks complete instantly (simultaneous animations)
   - Checkmarks appear rapidly
   - Counter zooms to emphasize speed
4. **Time Savings Calculation (20–32 sec)** — Bold stat appears
   - "60 seconds" (large, bold)
   - vs. "90+ minutes"
   - "That's 10+ hours saved every week"
5. **CTA (32–38 sec)** — "Try free at enlista.ai"

**Audio:**
- Clock ticking sound on left (slow, methodical)
- Fast "whoosh" sound on right (conveying speed)
- Background music — dynamic, energetic, builds toward the payoff
- Optional voiceover: "Spend less time on paperwork, more time on selling."

**Text Overlays:**
- "90+ minutes" (left side)
- "60 seconds" (right side)
- "That's 10+ hours per week"
- "Time to focus on what matters."
- CTA: "Try free at enlista.ai"

**Animation Techniques:**
- Split-screen layout (canvas divided 50/50)
- Time counters (numbers ticking down, with easing to show speed difference)
- Staggered task appearance (left) vs. simultaneous (right)
- Scale animation on final stat (emphasis)
- Color coding (red/slow vs. green/fast)

### Reel 4: Lead Response Race
**Duration:** 42 seconds  
**Format:** 1080×1920px (16:9 portrait)  
**Hook:** "First to reply wins the lead."

**Visual Flow:**
1. **Setup (0–4 sec)** — Two agents on-screen (illustrated or iconographic)
   - "Agent A" (left, no Enlista)
   - "Agent B" (right, with Enlista)
2. **Lead Inquiry (4–8 sec)** — Notification appears, comes to both agents simultaneously
   - Lead message: "I'm interested in the downtown apartment. What's the price?"
3. **Agent A: Manual Response (8–22 sec)** — Slow-motion effect
   - Thinking bubble (takes 10+ seconds)
   - Typing slowly (5 seconds per word)
   - Message construction visible but laborious
   - Counter: "15+ seconds... still typing..."
4. **Agent B: Enlista Response (8–18 sec)** — Fast-forward effect
   - AI icon appears
   - Message generated instantly
   - Sent immediately
   - Counter: "2 seconds... sent!"
5. **Outcome (22–34 sec)** — Agent B wins the meeting
   - Calendar notification appears for Agent B
   - Checkmark: "Meeting scheduled"
   - Agent A's message finally sends (but too late)
   - Fade Agent A (metaphorically loses the lead)
6. **Impact Statement (34–40 sec)** — Text overlay
   - "Respond first. Win the meeting."
   - "Book the deal. Close the sale."
7. **CTA (40–42 sec)** — "Contact us / Try free at enlista.ai"

**Audio:**
- Slow/cautious music on left (Agent A struggling)
- Upbeat, triumphant music on right (Agent B winning)
- Notification sounds for incoming message
- Whoosh/success sound when Agent B books meeting
- Background music — competitive, energetic, high-tempo

**Text Overlays:**
- "First to reply wins."
- "Agent A: 15+ seconds"
- "Agent B: 2 seconds"
- "Meeting booked."
- "Respond instantly. Win the deal."
- CTA: "Contact us for setup / Try free at enlista.ai"

**Animation Techniques:**
- Slow-motion effect on Agent A (video playback speed modifier)
- Fast-forward/time-lapse effect on Agent B
- Dual counters (competing visually)
- Calendar/checkmark animation on victory
- Fade/dim Agent A side on outcome (subtle, shows loss)
- Dynamic background colors emphasizing speed difference

## Production Details

### Assets Required
- **Screenshots:** Dashboard listing form, generated output cards, WhatsApp automation page
- **11 Labs API:** Rachel voice (professional, clear) for Reel 1 narration
- **Background Music:** 4 licensed tracks (or royalty-free alternatives)
  - Reel 1: Upbeat, modern, energetic
  - Reel 2: Warm, professional, conversational
  - Reel 3: Dynamic, builds tension, celebratory payoff
  - Reel 4: Competitive, urgent, triumphant
- **Color Palette:** Enlista brand colors (blue #1D4ED8, green #059669, amber #D97706, red #DC2626)

### Technology Stack
- **Remotion:** React-based video rendering for animations, text, mockups
- **11 Labs API:** Voice synthesis (Reel 1)
- **FFmpeg:** Audio/video mixing
- **Node.js:** Orchestration and asset management

### Export Specifications
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 video codec, AAC audio)
- **Resolution:** 1080×1920px (16:9 portrait)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps
- **Bitrate:** 5000–8000 kbps (optimized for Instagram)
- **Duration:** 35–42 seconds per Reel
- **Audio:** Mixed (voice + background music + sound effects)

### Tone & Messaging
All Reels emphasize **benefit-focused messaging** aligned with existing Enlista marketing:
- Speed and efficiency (60 seconds, instant responses)
- Bilingual quality (native Arabic, not Google Translate)
- Automation and lead nurturing (WhatsApp follows-up, lead scoring)
- ROI and time savings (10+ hours per week)
- Professional, conversational tone (questions and direct pain-point acknowledgment)

### CTAs
- **Reels 1 & 3:** "Try free at enlista.ai"
- **Reels 2 & 4:** "Contact us" (setup/demo required) + optional "Try free at enlista.ai"

## Success Criteria

Each Reel should:
1. ✅ Load quickly on Instagram (optimized filesize and bitrate)
2. ✅ Capture attention in first 3 seconds (strong hook)
3. ✅ Demonstrate a real product benefit (not just aesthetic)
4. ✅ Use real assets (dashboard screenshots, 11 Labs voice)
5. ✅ Match brand tone (conversational, benefit-focused, professional)
6. ✅ Drive action (CTAs clear and compelling)
7. ✅ Perform well across mobile devices (tested on Instagram)
