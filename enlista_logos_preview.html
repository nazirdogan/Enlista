Enlista Logo Concepts

Five distinct logo options — same brand color palette

Option 1

The Property Pin

A building grid inside a location pin — communicates real estate + location at a glance. Great for app icons and favicons.

Option 2

The List Lettermark

The letter "E" built from listing-style bullet lines inside a rounded square. Ties the name "Enlista" directly to its core function — managing listings.

Option 3

Smart Home

A clean house silhouette paired with AI sparkle accents and an "AI" badge. Instantly communicates AI-powered real estate technology.

Option 4

Portal Sync

Three fanned bars represent multi-portal syndication — Enlista's core value prop of syncing listings across platforms. The tagline reinforces the workflow.

Option 5

The Badge

A professional circular emblem with a geometric "E" and text cursor — blends authority with tech. Works well for watermarks, stamps, and formal contexts.
