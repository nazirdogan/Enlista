## Sending Notes

- **Send window**: Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM Gulf Standard Time (UTC+4)
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, UAE public holidays
- **From name**: Use a personal name, not a company name ("Nazir from ListingAI" > "ListingAI Team")
- **Signature**: Keep it minimal — name, company, one link (Calendly or website)
- **Tone check**: Read every email aloud. If it sounds like a sales pitch, rewrite it.
- **Volume**: Start with Tier A (80+ score) first. Don't blast — send 10–15/day max while testing.
- **CRM risk accounts**: Always verify manually before sending — one wrong assumption kills the pitch.
