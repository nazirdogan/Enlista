Sending Notes
Timing
  • Send window: Tue–Thu, 9–11 AM Gulf Standard Time (UTC+4)
  • Avoid: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, UAE public holidays
  • Best single day: Thursday (6.87% reply rate peak)
  • Wait the full 3 days before follow-up 1 — it increases response by up to 31%
Sender Setup
  • From name: Personal name only — "Nazir from ListingAI" not "ListingAI Team"
  • Signature: Name, company, one link (Calendly or website)
  • No HTML: Plain text only — images and links kill deliverability
  • One link max per email
Volume & Sequencing
  • Start with Tier A (score 80+) — MH, THEBROKS, Almira, The Capital Avenue
  • Max 10–15 sends/day while warming up domain
  • CRM risk accounts: verify manually before sending — don't assume manual workflow
  • LinkedIn-only contacts: standard sequence won't apply — use DM variant
Tone Check
  • Read every email aloud before sending
  • If it sounds like a sales pitch, rewrite it
  • Avoid: "synergy," "leverage," "best-in-class," "circle back"
  • Avoid: "I hope this email finds you well" as an opener
  • You → them ratio should be 2:1 minimum
Reply Handling
Positive Reply ("1" or interested)
  • Respond within 2 hours max
  • Send Calendly link for 15-min demo
  • Move to demo-booked sequence
  • Mark as removed from sequence
Re-queue Reply ("2" or not now)
  • Acknowledge and thank them
  • Add to 90-day re-queue list
  • Note the re-contact date
  • Remove from current sequence
Opt-out ("3" or stop)
  • Honour it — do not contact again
  • Remove from all sequences
  • Mark in CRM as "do not contact"
No Reply (completed sequence)
  • Move to 6-month dormant list
  • Can re-engage with fresh angle in Q3 2026
  • Do not restart same sequence
