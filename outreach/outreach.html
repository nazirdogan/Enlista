WhatsApp Outreach Strategy
UAE is a WhatsApp-first B2B culture. For direct mobile contacts these rules make or break the outreach.
📱
When WA beats email
UAE founders and CEOs regularly handle deals over WhatsApp. For direct mobiles — especially where no email exists — it's often the faster and more natural channel.
⏱️
Send window
Tue–Thu, 10 AM–12 PM GST. Avoid Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, UAE public holidays, and weekends unless contact is already established.
📏
Message length
3–5 lines max on mobile. If it requires scrolling, it's too long. No bullet points. No headers. Write like a peer, not a pitch deck.
🔢
3 messages max
Stop after message 3 if no reply. More than that crosses into harassment. Move to email or LinkedIn and do not WhatsApp again.
🎙️
No cold voice notes
Voice notes are for established relationships only. Text only for initial outreach — voice is fine once they've replied.
💼
Use WhatsApp Business
Set up a WhatsApp Business account for ListingAI with your company name, logo, and website. Lets contacts verify who they're talking to before replying.
Do's and Don'ts
Do
  • Use a real name — "Nazir from ListingAI"
  • Lead with their business, not your product
  • Keep the ask ultra-low-friction ("worth a quick call?")
  • Send at 10 AM–12 PM GST, Tue–Thu
  • Wait at least 4 days before follow-up
  • Use WhatsApp Business with a complete profile
  • Stop after 3 messages if no reply
  • Honour any "not interested" response permanently
  • Reference something specific to their agency
Don't
  • Send cold voice notes
  • Open with "Hi, my name is X and I work at Y"
  • Use bullet points or headers
  • Send the same message as your email
  • Follow up more than twice after initial contact
  • Send on Friday afternoons or weekends
  • Add them to a WhatsApp group without consent
  • Send media files (PDFs, images) cold
  • Use "I hope this message finds you well"
