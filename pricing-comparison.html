05 — Verdict
Final Recommendation

Structure 3 is the right model.
Structure 1 is the right launch.

Structure 3 — per-agent pricing with individual accounts under a brokerage umbrella — is the correct long-term architecture. It mirrors how the UAE market already thinks (PropSpace charges per user), it captures revenue growth automatically as agencies hire, and it gives agents the individual ownership they need while giving brokerages the oversight they require for compliance.

The Solo plan is identical in both structures at $49/mo — so launch it today, collect real customers, and use that MRR to fund building the umbrella dashboard. Never sell annual contracts on Structure 1 flat tiers: the moment you do, you've locked yourself out of a clean migration.

  • Add brokerage waitlist CTA on the pricing page
